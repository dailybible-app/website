Delete Your Daily Bible App Account

If you would like to delete your account and associated data, you can do so directly in the app.

How to Delete Your Account

  1. Open the Daily Bible App
  2. Go to Profile
  3. Tap Settings
  4. Scroll to the bottom
  5. Tap Delete Account
  6. Confirm the deletion

Once confirmed, your account and associated personal data will be permanently deleted.
