Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 19 January 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Daily Bible App. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding data collection, use, and disclosure when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly to us, information that is automatically collected when you use our services, and information collected by third-party services integrated into our app.

2.1 Information You Provide:

  • Account Information: Email address, display name, profile photo (if provided)
  • Authentication Data: Information from third-party authentication providers (Google Sign-In, Apple Sign-In)
  • Reading Preferences: Preferred Bible version, preferred language, notification preferences, theme preferences (dark/light mode), font size, line spacing
  • Reading Data: Favorite verses, reading progress, bookmarks, notes, highlights (with color preferences), reading history, daily verse history, saved verse collections
  • User-Generated Content: Personal notes on verses, prayer journal entries, verse highlights, custom collections
  • Subscription Information: Pro subscription status, purchase history, transaction identifiers
  • Payment Information: Purchase transactions are processed by Google Play Store or Apple App Store. We do not collect or store your payment card details. Payment data is handled by the respective platform stores according to their privacy policies.

2.2 Automatically Collected Information:

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, app version, device model, screen resolution
  • Usage Data: Features used, reading activity, app interactions, time spent in app, pages viewed
  • Technical Data: IP address, device identifiers, crash reports, performance metrics, app launch times
  • Timestamps: Account creation date, last active date, reading session times
  • Local Storage: Bible text data (public domain versions stored locally), cached verses, reading progress (stored securely on your device)

2.3 Information Collected by Google Mobile Ads SDK:

Our app uses Google Mobile Ads SDK to display advertisements to free users. The Google Mobile Ads SDK automatically collects the following data:

  • IP Address: Used for general location estimation and ad delivery
  • Device Identifiers: Android Advertising ID (AdID), App Set ID, device identifiers
  • User Product Interactions: App launches, ad views, ad clicks, video views, taps, and other interactions
  • Diagnostic and Performance Data: App launch times, crashes, hang rates, energy usage, performance metrics
  • Advertising Data: Information about which ads were shown, clicked, or viewed

This data is collected automatically by Google Mobile Ads SDK and is used for:

  • Delivering and personalizing advertisements
  • Measuring ad performance and effectiveness
  • Fraud prevention and security
  • Analytics and reporting

You can control ad personalization through your device settings. On Android, you can reset or disable your Advertising ID in your device settings. Ads shown to Pro users are automatically disabled.

2.4 Information from Third-Party Services:

  • API.Bible: When using certain Bible versions (e.g., NIV), we may make API calls to API.Bible service. This may involve transmitting your IP address and device information to API.Bible for service delivery. Please review API.Bible's privacy policy for their data practices.
  • Firebase Services: Firebase may collect device information, usage data, and crash reports as part of their service provision.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

3.1 Service Provision:

  • To provide and maintain our services, including Bible reading, daily verses, devotionals, reading plans, and all app features
  • To authenticate your identity and manage your account
  • To personalize your experience, including saving your reading preferences, progress, notes, highlights, and bookmarks
  • To sync your data across devices using your account
  • To provide Pro features to users who have purchased Pro subscription
  • To manage in-app purchases and subscription status

3.2 Communication:

  • To send you notifications (with your consent) for daily readings and reminders
  • To provide customer support and respond to your inquiries
  • To send important service updates and announcements

3.3 Service Improvement:

  • To improve our services by analyzing usage patterns and feature adoption
  • To fix bugs, crashes, and performance issues
  • To develop new features and functionality
  • To optimize app performance and user experience

3.4 Advertising (Free Users Only):

  • To display relevant advertisements through Google Mobile Ads SDK
  • To measure ad performance and effectiveness
  • Pro users do not see advertisements

3.5 Security and Legal:

  • To ensure security and prevent fraud
  • To verify in-app purchases and prevent unauthorized access
  • To comply with legal obligations and respond to legal requests
  • To enforce our Terms of Service
